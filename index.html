Welcome

Where Every Glass Tells a Story

Nestled at 107 Lark Lane, Keith's Food & Wine Bar has been a cornerstone of Liverpool's vibrant social scene. We're more than a wine bar — we're a gathering place where friends become family, where the music is live, and where every bottle is chosen with passion.

Whether you're here for a solo evening with a glass of Carmenere, a group celebration with cocktails, or a midweek jazz night, Keith's offers the perfect backdrop — casual, cosy, and always welcoming.
